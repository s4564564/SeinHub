Game Overview

This is a permadeath multiplayer campaign where soldiers and warriors fight for the control of the founding titan. Dying quickly is a common thing here, only the strong and lucky ones survive.

The campaign is not a exact recreation of the aot storyline, but a similar one. It's divided in multiple arcs that contain multiple stages.

GAME FEATURES

Core mechanics and gameplay systems

Warriors Titan Shifters

Being Able BID to Transform into one of the three Titans in Warriors team.

Soldiers Titan Shifters

Being Able to Transform into one of the two titans RANDOMLY in Soldiers Team.

ODM Gear System

Being Able to Fly with the odm gear just like in the Attack On Titan Anime

Titan Fight PVP

Being Able to Fight others titan shifters, Warriors VS Soldiers..
